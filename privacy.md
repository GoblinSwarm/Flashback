Flashback Privacy Policy

Flashback does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All processing performed by the extension occurs locally in the user's browser. The extension does not send any information to external servers.

Flashback does not track users, collect analytics, or store browsing activity.

The extension only interacts with the video element present on the page in order to provide instant replay functionality.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the project repository.
